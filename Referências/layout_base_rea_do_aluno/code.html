Lesson Overview

In this lesson, we dive deep into the principles of balance and flow on a scrapbook page. You'll learn how to anchor your focal points using subtle layers of patterned paper and solid cardstock. We'll explore the 'Rule of Thirds' specifically adapted for 12x12 layouts, ensuring your memories take center stage without feeling cluttered.
